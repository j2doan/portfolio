About Me

My name is Jacob Doan. It is a pleasure to meet you. I am a third-year undergraduate currently pursuing a B.S. in Data Science at UC San Diego. With a strong interest in computer science, AI, and statistics, I discovered Data Science to be the perfect blend of those fields. I have the freedom to explore serious issues like surgical anesthesia modeling and theory of mind, to more casual interests like RNG probabilities in gaming, restaurant trends, or whatever happens to catch my curiosity on that day!
